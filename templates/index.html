Why?

Now that the arms race of AR/VR technology has officaly taken off, I wanted to find out how Meta and Apple are approaching their visions for the technology. To do this I used NYT's API to scrape data of their website/articles to better understand the impact of how each company is using terminology and marketing to entice the mass markets to their product and ecosystem. I hope you enjoy!

Metaverse?
Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR) represent groundbreaking technologies with vast potential. However, their diverse terminology and interpretations can sometimes lead to confusion, as different companies and individuals define them in various ways.
VR/AR
Most companies commonly refer to virtual reality headsets as VR headsets or AR headsets. However, Apple and Meta have taken a different approach.
Meta (Facebook)
Facebook rebranded itself as Meta, drew inspiration from the science fiction concept of the metaverse their vision of a virtual realm for work and play.
Apple
Apple has coined its VR/AR headsets as "spatial computers," transforming them into virtual computers that seamlessly integrate into your surroundings.
